{{ activeCard.title }} {{ activeCard.details}} Close About this module This module explores the intersection of Indigenous knowledge, maritime commerce, and whale protection. Each card in the module presents a case study or research article exploring how Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK) interacts with environmental governance (particularly, whale protection) and/or maritime commerce. The focus is primarily on how TEK can be beneficial to environmental governance, but also includes potential conflicts with Western environmentalism.
